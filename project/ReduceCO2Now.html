What We Can Do

Solutions That Work

Stabilizing CO₂ is possible — but requires coordinated action at scale. These are proven levers. We can pull all of them simultaneously.

01 / Energy
Reduce Fossil Fuel Use
Transition to renewables, increase carbon pricing, and shift incentives away from oil, gas and coal. The single largest lever available.
02 / Carbon
Bury Biomass & Capture Carbon
Biochar, enhanced weathering, and direct air capture can actively remove CO₂ already in the atmosphere — not just slow new emissions.
03 / Food
Eat a CO₂-Optimized Diet
Food production accounts for ~25% of global emissions. Shifting to plant-rich diets and choosing local foods makes a measurable difference.
04 / Waste
Reduce Food Waste
~1/3 of all food produced globally is wasted, creating a massive hidden carbon footprint. Cutting waste is high-impact and immediate.
05 / Consumption
Cut Overconsumption
Manufacturing, shipping and disposing of goods drives significant emissions. Buying less, buying smarter, extending product lifetimes all help.
06 / Policy
Carbon Pricing & Policy
Make fossil fuels reflect their true cost. Carbon taxes and cap-and-trade systems have proven track records in reducing emissions at scale.
